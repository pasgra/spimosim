About the model

A modified version of the Bornholdt model [1] where the spins can take values from -1 to 1. The absolute value of the spins is the probability with which it got chosen to the power of Î³. 

[1] S. Bornholdt, Expectation bubbles in a spin model of markets: Intermittency from
frustration across scales, Int. J. Mod. Phys. C, Vol. 12, No. 5 (2001) 667-674.
