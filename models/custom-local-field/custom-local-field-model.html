About the model

This model is based on the Bornholdt-Modell [1]

The local field h is an function of the global magnetization M∈[-1, 1], the local magnetization l∈[-1, 1] and the spin s∈{-1,1}. 

[1] S. Bornholdt, Expectation bubbles in a spin model of markets: Intermittency from
frustration across scales, Int. J. Mod. Phys. C, Vol. 12, No. 5 (2001) 667-674.
